How to Play


1. Hold down a key amongst the 1-6 keys on your keyboard.
2. Wait for the matching notes to pass over the fretboard
3. Click the "Strum!" button with your mouse.
You lose HP when you mistime a strum, strum the wrong notes, or allow the notes to hit the bottom of the screen.

You win when you get enough points, or lose when your HP depletes to 0.
